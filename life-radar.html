Privacy Policy for LifeRadar

Last updated: May 8, 2026

Developer

This application is developed by YOQYOQ.

Microphone Data

LifeRadar uses your device microphone to receive ultrasonic sound wave signals for life detection analysis.

  • Microphone data is processed in real-time on your device only.
  • We do NOT record, store, or upload any audio data.
  • Audio data is immediately discarded after analysis is complete.

Sensor Data

LifeRadar accesses device sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope, magnetometer) for vibration detection and navigation purposes.

  • All sensor data is processed locally on your device.
  • Sensor data is never transmitted to external servers.

No Data Collection

LifeRadar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No user accounts are required.
  • No analytics or tracking services are used.
  • No personal information is gathered.

Advertisements

Free users may see advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. You can remove all advertisements with a one-time in-app purchase.

In-App Purchases

Premium features are available via a one-time purchase ($3.99). Purchase records are managed by Google Play and are not shared with the developer beyond what is necessary for purchase verification.

Permissions Used

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — Required for receiving ultrasonic sound signals.
  • Vibration (VIBRATE) — Used for haptic feedback during scanning.
  • Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK) — Keeps the screen on during active scanning.
  • Activity Recognition — Step counting for navigation to detected targets.
  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS) — Optional notification alerts.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
